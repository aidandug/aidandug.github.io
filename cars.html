My Cars

BMW 328i

The 2013 BMW 328i is equipped with a 2.0-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder engine, producing 240 horsepower and 255 lb-ft of torque. It offers a smooth balance of luxury and performance, featuring a rear-wheel drive setup, 8-speed automatic transmission, and an impressive 0-60 mph acceleration in just 5.8 seconds.

Mustang GT

The 6th-generation Mustang, the S550, is powered by Ford’s 3rd-gen Coyote engine—a 5.0-liter V8 delivering 460 horsepower and 420 lb-ft of torque. It rockets from 0-60 mph in just 4 seconds and features a 6-speed manual transmission. Known for its aggressive design and exhilarating exhaust note, this car is a true performance machine. To take it even further, I’ve personally installed BC Racing Coilovers, improving cornering stability and responsiveness, making it not just a straight-line beast but a joy to drive through twists and turns.
